<h4>How anonymous are my posts?</h4>
No information about the poster is visible by other users.
<hr>
<h4>How do i know if a poster is a staff member?</h4>
Posts made by staff members have the names highlighted in purple and have their role included after two hashes (<font color=purple><b>staff_user</b> ## admin </font>)

<hr>

<h4>How do i format text?</h4>
<ul>
<li> **bold** - <b>bold</b></li>
<li> *italic* - <i>italic</i></li>
<li>>greentext - <font class=greentext>>greentext</font></li>
<li>&#60;orangetext - <font class=orangetext>&#60;orangetext</font></li>
<li>^bluetext - <font class=bluetext>^bluetext</font></li>
</ul>
<br>
Links are formatted automatically
